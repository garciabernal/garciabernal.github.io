Julia codes

These are some Julia packages I’ve created for my work, shared here in case they’re useful. Suggestions are welcome!

  • DataReader.jl: A package for reading and importing datasets into Julia. The following packages are built on top of it:
    • ReadEFF.jl: Read the Encuesta Financiera de las Familias (EFF) from the Banco de España.
    • ReadNLSY.jl: Read the National Longitudinal Surveys of Youth (NLSY) from the BLS.
    • ReadSCF.jl: Read the Survey of Consumer Finances (SCF) from the Federal Reserve.
